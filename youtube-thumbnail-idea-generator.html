How to Create Clickable Thumbnails

1. Use High Contrast Colors

Bright colors against dark backgrounds grab attention in YouTube's interface.

2. Include Faces with Emotion

Thumbnails with expressive faces get more clicks - show surprise, excitement, or curiosity.

3. Add Text Overlay

Use bold, easy-to-read text that complements your title and creates curiosity.

4. Follow Trending Styles

Analyze top-performing videos in your niche and adapt successful thumbnail patterns.
