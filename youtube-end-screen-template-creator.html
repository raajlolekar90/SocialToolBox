How to Use Your Downloaded Template

1

Download Your Template

After creating your end screen template, click the "Download Template" button to save the design to your computer. The template will be downloaded as a ZIP file containing all necessary assets.

Download button interface

2

Extract the ZIP File

Locate the downloaded file on your computer and extract its contents. You should find:

  • End screen image template (PNG or PSD)
  • Instruction file (PDF or TXT)
  • Optional: Custom branding assets
3

Open YouTube Studio

Log in to your YouTube account and navigate to YouTube Studio. Select the video where you want to add the end screen.

YouTube Studio interface

4

Add End Screen Element

In the video editor, scroll to the end screen section. Click "Add Element" and choose the type of element you want to add (video, subscribe, playlist, etc.).

5

Upload Your Template Background

If your template includes a background image, upload it to the end screen editor. Position your interactive elements according to the template design.

Pro Tips for Effective End Screens

  • Keep your end screen visible for at least 5 seconds to give viewers time to interact
  • Use high-contrast colors for buttons to make them stand out
  • Include a clear call-to-action (e.g., "Subscribe" or "Watch Next")
  • Test your end screen on different devices to ensure proper visibility
  • Update your end screens periodically to promote new content

How to Create Effective End Screens

1. Include Key Elements

Add a subscribe button, relevant video suggestions, and a channel promotion to maximize engagement.

2. Keep It Simple

Avoid clutter. Use 3-4 elements maximum to keep your end screen clean and effective.

3. Strategic Placement

Position elements where they're easily clickable but don't obstruct important video content.

4. Consistent Branding

Use colors and styles that match your channel's branding for a professional look.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why are end screens important for YouTube videos?

End screens help retain viewers by directing them to more of your content, increasing watch time and subscriptions. They're the last impression you make on viewers, so they're crucial for channel growth.

How many elements should I include in my end screen?

YouTube allows up to four elements in an end screen. We recommend using 3-4 relevant elements such as a subscribe button, a video suggestion, and a channel link to maximize engagement without overcrowding.

Can I use the same end screen for all my videos?

While you can use similar elements, it's best to customize end screens for each video. Suggest relevant content that viewers might enjoy based on the video they just watched.

How long should the end screen appear in my video?

YouTube recommends allowing 5-20 seconds for your end screen. This gives viewers enough time to engage with the elements without dragging out the end of your video.
